Our Legacy

The World Taekwondo Academy was first established in South Korea in 1973 by Grand Master Woon Se Lee after retiring from the Korean army. He moved to the USA in 1989 with his family and opened World Taekwondo Academy in Olney, Maryland.

Got Questions? We Got Answers

Everything you need to know

What types of classes do you offer?

We offer Taekwondo classes for kids, teens, and adults, focusing on traditional and Olympic styles. We also have a special trial program for new students.

What should my child wear to their first class?

New students should wear comfortable athletic clothing. Once enrolled, they will need a Taekwondo uniform (dobok) that can be purchased through our school.

Do I need previous martial arts experience to join?

No previous experience is required! Our classes cater to all skill levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

How can I sign up for the trial program?

You can sign up for our special trial program by contacting us directly via phone or email. We’ll provide you with details on our current offers.

What are the benefits of practicing Taekwondo?

Taekwondo helps improve physical fitness, strength, flexibility, and coordination. It also promotes self-discipline, confidence, and self-defense skills.

Are there any competitions or events?

Yes, we encourage our students to participate in local competitions and events to showcase their skills and progress. We also host in-house events to celebrate our students’ achievements.
